Project Description

Here at Kickstart we are dedicated to serving as a guide to those both interested and willing to learn computer literacy, especially those without the resources to do so.

What is Kickstart?

  • We are both a student created service and program.

What is our goal?

  • Our goal is to provide our largest possible means of guidance for those without access to sufficient resources in attaining computer literacy.
